# 01 — Problem

The full problem statement lives at [`docs/methodology/01-problem.md`](../../../docs/methodology/01-problem.md) in this repository.

In short: when an AI is given a Foundation and asked to brainstorm, plan, or generate code, it tends to reach for the ultimate goal in *one big inference*, filling unanswered blanks of the path with its own reasoning. The same failure mode reproduces at three depths (surface, depth 1, depth 2). The `defining-foundation` skill exists to keep the destination clean from path-level inference.

Read the canonical document for the full diagnosis.
